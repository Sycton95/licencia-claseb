# Content and Study Mode Notes

## Manual source

- The study material is based on the official Clase B manual and other formal public sources.
- The app should continue treating official documents as reference material, not as a private question bank replacement.

## Public study mode policy

- A future `Estudio` mode may include an in-app PDF viewer or guided reading experience.
- That feature must not ship until the product documents the exact source of the PDF/manual used in the app.
- Before release, the team must decide whether the app is:
  - embedding the PDF,
  - redistributing a copy,
  - or linking to an official hosted document.

## Donations and monetization caution

- The current product scope is a public practice app, not a paid content product.
- If donations or monetization are added later, the content/legal posture must be reviewed again before exposing a PDF-based study mode.
- Until that review is documented, no public PDF-reading feature should be released.
